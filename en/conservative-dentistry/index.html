What is Conservative Dentistry?

Conservative dentistry deals with restoring the hard tissues of the tooth that have been damaged by cavities, trauma, or developmental alterations. The main objective is to conserve as much healthy tooth as possible through minimally invasive treatments.

In our clinic we use state-of-the-art materials, such as high-aesthetic composite resins and ceramics, which not only restore functionality to the tooth but also mimic its natural appearance.

Acting on time is key. Early conservative treatment prevents damage from progressing and requiring more complex solutions such as root canals or extractions. Our approach is based on precise diagnosis and timely intervention.

"The best tooth is your own. We work to keep it healthy and functional for as long as possible."
